Choropleth maps are good for showing data in an easy to understand way.
Choropleth maps made in python are interactive.

The GDP per capita project shows how much the gross domestic product is per inhabitant in different countries.
Holding over a country will show the GDP per capita of that country, the name of the country and its placement in the list.
If it doesn't show anything, it's because the data of GDP where not published.
Data collected from wikipedia: "List of countries by GDP (PPP) per capita".
